Yes. We manage planning, allocation, audience testing, and optimization. You keep full transparency on spend, returns, and channel-level performance.

You get a live dashboard plus weekly updates. Each month we deliver a strategic performance report with what changed, why, and what we will optimize next.

Absolutely. We align lead definitions, handoff rules, and funnel KPIs with your sales process so both teams optimize for revenue, not just clicks.

Access to analytics, ad accounts, and your website CMS, plus a clear business objective. We handle the audit and build the growth roadmap from there.
