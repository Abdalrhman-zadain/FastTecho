FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Which e-commerce platform do you recommend?

It depends on your needs. Shopify is great for quick launches, WooCommerce for WordPress users, and headless commerce (Next.js + Medusa/Saleor) for maximum performance and flexibility. We'll recommend the best fit during our discovery call.

Can you migrate my existing store?

Yes. We handle full migrations including products, customers, orders, reviews, and SEO redirects. We ensure zero data loss and minimal downtime during the transition.

Do you support local payment methods?

Absolutely. We integrate regional payment gateways (Tap, HyperPay, Fawry, Mada) alongside international options like Stripe and PayPal, plus cash-on-delivery workflows.

How do you handle shipping integration?

We integrate with major shipping providers (Aramex, DHL, FedEx, local couriers) with real-time rate calculation, tracking updates, and automated label printing.

Can the store scale as my business grows?

Yes. We build with scalability in mind — from handling 100 products to 100,000+. Our architecture supports traffic spikes, multi-region CDNs, and horizontal scaling as your business expands.

Do you provide marketing and SEO for the store?

Every store includes technical SEO foundations. For ongoing marketing, we offer add-on services including content strategy, paid ads management, email campaigns, and conversion rate optimization.
