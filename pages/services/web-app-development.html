FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take between 6–14 weeks depending on complexity. A simple website can be delivered in 3–4 weeks, while a full SaaS platform may take 12–20 weeks. We'll provide a clear timeline during the discovery phase.

Do I own the source code?

Yes — 100%. Once the project is completed and paid for, all source code, design files, and documentation are fully transferred to you. No vendor lock-in, ever.

What's your payment structure?

We typically work with a 40/30/30 model: 40% upfront to begin, 30% at the design milestone, and the final 30% upon delivery. For enterprise contracts, we offer flexible monthly billing.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely. We regularly embed with in-house teams as dedicated developers, designers, or project leads. We use your existing tools (Jira, Slack, GitHub, etc.) and adapt to your workflow.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes. Every package includes post-launch support (1–12 months depending on the plan). We handle bug fixes, security patches, performance monitoring, and feature updates.

What technologies do you specialize in?

Our core stack includes React, Next.js, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, Flutter, React Native, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AWS, and Docker. We choose the best tools for each project's unique needs.
